# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as members, contributors, and leaders, pledge to make participation in this project and our community a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone.  
We are committed to fostering a collaborative, inclusive, and harassment-free environment.

## Our Standards
To create a positive environment, we encourage:
- **Professionalism and Friendliness** in all communications.  
- **Respectful Collaboration**: Listen actively, acknowledge differing perspectives, and work together constructively.  
- **Constructive Feedback**: Share helpful insights that empower others to improve without being dismissive or hostile.  
- **Encouragement**: Celebrate contributions, both big and small, and support newcomers.  

We prohibit:
- Harassment or intimidation of any kind.  
- Hate speech, discrimination, or offensive remarks.  
- Spamming, trolling, or disruptive behavior.  
- Sharing of harmful, misleading, or malicious content.  

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies across all official Kudagon repositories and approved communication platforms.  
Currently, the primary platform for communication is this repository. Future platforms (e.g., Slack, Discord, etc.) will also fall under this Code of Conduct once approved.

## Consequences
The project team will determine and enforce consequences appropriate to the severity of the violation, which may include:
- A private warning.  
- Temporary or permanent restrictions from participation.  
- Removal from community spaces or repositories.  

## Commitment
We are dedicated to creating a positive, inclusive, and respectful community where everyone can contribute effectively and feel valued.
